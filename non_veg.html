Delicious Non-Veg Recipes from Around the World

Chicken Biryani (India)

Chicken Biryani is a royal Indian dish, famous for its aromatic rice and tender chicken. It’s a festive favorite across South Asia.

What You’ll Need:

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • Fresh coriander & mint leaves
  • Salt to taste
  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry onions until golden, then add chicken and cook.
  3. Add tomatoes, yogurt, and biryani masala; cook until chicken is tender.
  4. Boil rice separately until 70% cooked.
  5. Layer chicken and rice with herbs, cover, and steam (dum) for 20 minutes.

Sushi (Japan)

Sushi is a Japanese delicacy, known for its artful presentation and fresh flavors. It’s a symbol of Japanese culinary tradition.

What You’ll Need:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 4 tbsp rice vinegar
  • Nori seaweed sheets
  • Fresh salmon or tuna (sliced)
  • Cucumber & avocado (sliced)
  • Soy sauce & wasabi
  1. Cook sushi rice with water, then mix with rice vinegar.
  2. Place nori on a bamboo mat and spread rice evenly.
  3. Arrange fish, cucumber, and avocado on rice.
  4. Roll tightly and slice into pieces.
  5. Serve with soy sauce and wasabi.

Butter Chicken (India)

Butter Chicken is a creamy, mildly spiced curry that’s beloved in Indian restaurants worldwide. It’s a comfort food for many non-vegetarians.

What You’ll Need:

  • 500g chicken (boneless)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 onions (chopped)
  • 3 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup cream
  • Spices (garam masala, chili powder, salt)
  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices for 1 hour.
  2. Grill or pan-fry chicken pieces.
  3. In a pan, cook onions and tomato puree with butter.
  4. Add cream and spices, then stir in chicken.
  5. Simmer for 10 minutes and serve hot.

Beef Burger (USA)

The beef burger is an American icon, representing fast food culture and backyard barbecues. It’s enjoyed in countless variations globally.

What You’ll Need:

  • 500g ground beef
  • Salt and pepper
  • 4 burger buns
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion slices
  • Ketchup & mayonnaise
  1. Season beef with salt and pepper; form into patties.
  2. Grill patties until cooked to desired doneness.
  3. Toast burger buns lightly.
  4. Assemble with lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, and sauces.
  5. Serve immediately.

Roast Chicken (UK)

Roast chicken is a classic Sunday meal in the UK, known for its crispy skin and juicy meat. It’s a family favorite for gatherings.

What You’ll Need:

  • 1 whole chicken (about 1.5 kg)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh rosemary & thyme
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 lemon (halved)
  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Rub chicken with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Stuff cavity with lemon, garlic, and herbs.
  4. Roast for 1–1.5 hours until golden and cooked through.
  5. Rest for 10 minutes before carving.
