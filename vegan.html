Delicious Vegan Recipes from Around the World

Vegan cuisine is celebrated for its creativity and health benefits. Explore these global favorites!

Vegan Buddha Bowl

A colorful, nutrient-packed bowl that originated from the idea of balanced, mindful eating. Buddha Bowls are popular for their variety and vibrant presentation.

What You’ll Need:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup roasted chickpeas
  • 1 cup steamed broccoli
  • 1 cup roasted sweet potato
  • 1/2 avocado (sliced)
  • Tahini dressing

Your Path to Deliciousness

  1. Cook quinoa and set aside.
  2. Roast chickpeas with seasoning until crispy.
  3. Steam broccoli until tender.
  4. Roast sweet potato cubes until golden.
  5. Assemble bowl with all ingredients and drizzle with tahini.

Hummus with Pita Bread (Middle East)

Hummus is a staple in Middle Eastern cuisine, loved for its creamy texture and protein-rich chickpeas. It’s a centuries-old dip enjoyed worldwide.

What You’ll Need:

  • 1 can chickpeas (drained)
  • 3 tbsp tahini
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Pita bread

Your Path to Deliciousness

  1. Blend chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and olive oil until smooth.
  2. Adjust consistency with water if needed.
  3. Transfer to a bowl and drizzle with extra olive oil.
  4. Warm pita bread.
  5. Serve hummus with pita slices.

Vegan Pad Thai (Thailand)

Pad Thai is Thailand’s most famous noodle dish, and the vegan version is just as flavorful and satisfying, packed with tofu and fresh veggies.

What You’ll Need:

  • 200g rice noodles
  • 1 cup tofu cubes
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1 carrot (julienned)
  • Pad Thai sauce (soy sauce, tamarind, sugar)
  • Chopped peanuts

Your Path to Deliciousness

  1. Cook rice noodles as per instructions.
  2. Pan-fry tofu until golden.
  3. Sauté carrots and bean sprouts briefly.
  4. Add noodles and Pad Thai sauce; toss well.
  5. Garnish with peanuts and serve.

Lentil Soup

Lentil soup is a comfort food found in many cultures, prized for its heartiness and nutritional value. It’s a go-to vegan classic!

What You’ll Need:

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 carrots (chopped)
  • 2 celery stalks (chopped)
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Spices (cumin, paprika, salt, pepper)

Your Path to Deliciousness

  1. Sauté onion, carrots, and celery in a pot.
  2. Add lentils and vegetable broth.
  3. Season with spices.
  4. Simmer until lentils are soft.
  5. Blend partially for a creamy texture.

Vegan Tacos

Vegan tacos bring together the bold flavors of Mexican cuisine with plant-based ingredients, making them a fun and healthy meal for everyone.

What You’ll Need:

  • 6 small corn tortillas
  • 1 cup black beans
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 avocado (diced)
  • Fresh cilantro

Your Path to Deliciousness

  1. Warm tortillas in a pan.
  2. Mix black beans, corn, and tomatoes in a bowl.
  3. Fill tortillas with the mixture.
  4. Top with avocado and cilantro.
  5. Serve immediately.
